The coronavirus disease (COVID-19) pandemic led to the global implementation of measures to reduce COVID-19 virus transmission. This affected other communicable diseases including influenza, whose circulation decreased worldwide in 2020. In Cambodia, fewer influenza cases were detected while COVID-19 restrictions were in place. The country experienced outbreaks of influenza from June 2020, after the Ministry of Health began to ease COVID-19 restrictions.
